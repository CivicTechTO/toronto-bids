# TRCA — permission to archive public bid listings

**Body:** Toronto and Region Conservation Authority (TRCA)
**Portal:** https://trca.bidsandtenders.ca/
**Granted:** 2026-07-18, by the TRCA Procurement Department (procurement@trca.ca)
**Gate flipped in:** the commit that adds this file (`config.BIDS_TENDERS_PORTALS` → `trca` `enabled: True`).

## What it covers

Periodic, read-only, rate-limited fetching of the **publicly visible listing metadata**
on trca.bidsandtenders.ca (solicitation number, title, status, posted/closing dates), for
the toronto-bids public civic archive, with attribution to TRCA. **Not** covered: logging
in, downloading bid documents, or anything requiring a registered account — those remain
out of scope (the Vendor clickwrap), unchanged by this grant.

TRCA's conditions (honour these in the implementation):
- **Rate-limiting:** keep the nightly fetch low-impact so it does not trip automated
  security/firewall blocks on the bidsandtenders platform.
- **Attribution:** attribute TRCA as the data source in the published archive.
- **Point of contact:** procurement@trca.ca — TRCA will reach out there on any technical
  issue; likewise report any unexpected blocks to them.

## The request that was sent

`docs/letters/2026-07-18-trca-portal-permission.md` — read-only, rate-limited periodic
fetch of publicly visible listing metadata; no login, no documents; attribution offered;
immediate cessation on request; Bill 97 (2027-02-01 amalgamation) cited as the reason for
urgency.
